Before You Reach Out

We typically respond to WhatsApp messages within a few hours during office hours, and to email or form submissions within 1-2 working days.

Walk-ins are welcome during office hours, but booking ahead via phone or WhatsApp helps us make sure a counsellor is available for you.

Select "Fee & Payment" as the subject in the form above, or use the Fee & Payment WhatsApp quick-chat button, and our accounts desk will assist you.

Our office is closed on Sundays, so messages sent then are answered the next working day (Monday).
