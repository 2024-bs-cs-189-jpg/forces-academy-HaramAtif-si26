Get Started

How to Apply

Joining Forces Academy Faisalabad is simple. Follow the steps below to secure your seat for the upcoming batch.

Contact Admissions Office
  • 1
    Visit or Call the Campus

    Reach out to our admissions desk in person or by phone to express interest.

  • 2
    Fill the Admission Form

    Complete the registration form with your personal and academic details.

  • 3
    Submit Required Documents

    Provide the documents listed below along with the completed form.

  • 4
    Attend the Placement Test

    Take a short assessment so we can place you in the right course level.

  • 5
    Pay Fee & Confirm Seat

    Complete the fee payment to confirm your seat in the chosen program.

Who Can Apply

Eligibility Criteria

Matric Science / Arts

Passed Class 8 with at least 60% marks. Open to boys and girls.

FSc Pre-Medical / Pre-Engineering

Passed Matric (Science group) with at least 60% marks.

Entry Test Preparation

Minimum FA/FSc (or equivalent) or currently enrolled in final year.

ISSB & Interview Prep

Must have qualified the relevant written entry test (Army / Navy / PAF).

Checklist

Required Documents

Please bring the following documents at the time of admission. Incomplete files may delay your seat confirmation.

  • Original + photocopy of matriculation certificate / marksheet
  • Original + photocopy of CNIC / B-Form
  • 4 recent passport-size photographs
  • School / college leaving certificate
  • Parent / guardian CNIC photocopy
  • Domicile certificate (for entry test / ISSB programs)
