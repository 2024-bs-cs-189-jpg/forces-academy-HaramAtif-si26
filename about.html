Our Story

A Legacy of Discipline & Distinction

Founded over 15 years ago, Forces Academy Faisalabad was established with a single mission: to guide talented young men and women of Faisalabad toward successful careers in the Pakistan Army, Air Force, and Navy. What began as a small coaching centre with a handful of dedicated instructors has grown into one of the region's most trusted institutes for defence forces preparation.

Over the years, we have refined our written test coaching, physical training regimen, and ISSB interview grooming into a structured, results-driven system. Today, our alumni serve with distinction across all three services, and our name has become synonymous with disciplined preparation and proven results.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide every cadet-aspirant in Faisalabad with disciplined, high-quality academic coaching, physical conditioning, and interview grooming — equipping them with the skills and confidence needed to succeed at every stage of the armed forces selection process.

Our Vision

To be recognised as Pakistan's leading defence-forces preparation academy — a name trusted by families across Punjab for producing disciplined, capable, and confident officers who serve the nation with honour.
