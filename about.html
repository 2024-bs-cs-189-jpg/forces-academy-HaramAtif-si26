Our Story

A Legacy of Discipline & Distinction

Founded over 15 years ago, Forces Academy Faisalabad began with a simple goal: to give aspiring cadets from across the region a genuine, structured path into the Pakistan Army, Air Force, and Navy. What started as a small coaching centre for written test preparation has grown into one of Faisalabad's most trusted institutes for defence forces admissions.

Over the years, thousands of candidates have passed through our classrooms and training grounds, guided by instructors who understand exactly what selection boards look for — from the written exam and physical tests to the rigor of the ISSB interview process. Today, our academy combines academic coaching, physical conditioning, and psychological grooming under one roof, continuing a legacy built on discipline, consistency, and results.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To prepare every cadet with the academic knowledge, physical fitness, and mental discipline required to succeed in Army, Air Force, and Navy selection processes, while instilling the values of integrity, resilience, and national service.

Our Vision

To be Faisalabad's leading institute for defence forces preparation, recognized for consistently producing confident, capable, and disciplined future officers who serve their nation with distinction.
