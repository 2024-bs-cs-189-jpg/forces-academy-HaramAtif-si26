Our Story

Building Pakistan's Next Generation of Defenders

Forces Academy Faisalabad was founded with a single purpose: to guide talented, disciplined young men and women through every stage of Army, Navy, and Air Force selection — from the written test to the final ISSB interview.

Over 15 years, our structured coaching, physical training, and personality grooming programs have helped thousands of candidates step confidently into the armed forces and other competitive services.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To equip every candidate with the academic knowledge, physical fitness, and mental discipline required to succeed in defence force selection — and in life beyond it.

Our Vision

To be Faisalabad's most trusted name in defence forces preparation, known for producing confident, capable, and principled future officers.
